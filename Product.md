# 🎯 Product Vision

Transform raw data into:

1. **Understanding** (What is this data?)
2. **Clarification** (What matters to the business?)
3. **Goals** (What should we optimize?)
4. **Insights** (What is happening?)
5. **Action** (What should we do next?)
